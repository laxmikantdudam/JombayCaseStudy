Business Case- Chocó Bliss International

The Situation

You are to assume the role of the recently appointed Regional Director in Chocó Bliss International (CBI), a growing global confectionary company. As part of CBI’s vision to expand its market share over the next 5 years, the Board is launching a new brand of chocolate bars in the country of Palzag in the next 9 months. This range of chocolate bars, which contains 7 different bars with 7 different flavors, should create a big impact with its target consumers, who it is intended will switch to this range from other competing products. 

The New Range

The 7 bars are as follows:

  1. Dark Chocolate
  2. Milk Chocolate
  3. White Chocolate
  4. Dark Chocolate with Mint
  5. Dark Chocolate with Orange
  6. Milk Chocolate with Peanut Butter
  7. Milk Chocolate with Various Nuts

This is a premium chocolate candy brand aimed predominantly at female consumers aged 25 years- 45 years. The brand character is modern, stylish and prestigious. The chocolate candy is longer lasting; it melts slowly in the mouth and has a smooth and creamy flavor that lingers. The cocoa beans are sourced from Mexico, Brazil and Peru.
